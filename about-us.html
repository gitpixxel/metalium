About Us

Who We Are

With experience of over 13 years, we are reputed manufacturers of magnificent exterior louvres and metal false ceiling systems. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility and in-house powder coating unit seek faultless quality and precision in redefining architectural landscapes. Powered by sustainability and a people-driven approach, excellence has been our way through which we have come to be trusted partners in transforming spaces into extra-ordinary experiences across India.

Our Journey so far

2010

Metalium's vision takes shape as a small team with big ambitions.

2013

Metalium embarks on its journey with a vision to redefine architectural aesthetics.

2015

A breakthrough design propelling Metalium into the spotlight.

2017

Successful collaboration on a landmark project establishes Metalium as a trusted partner for complex architectural challenges.

2019

Doubling our sales, we expand our reach and solidify our position as industry leaders.

Embracing advanced technology, we introduce groundbreaking products to meet evolving market demands.

  • Our Philosophy

    At Metalium, we believe that spaces should do more than just structures; they should elevate us, raise our lives to greater heights, and give us fulfillment in living. Our designs focus on well-being, productivity, and aesthetic appeal. We are committed to delivering high-quality, low-maintenance products that stand the test of time. Our unwavering focus on innovation ensures our solutions not only meet but exceed expectations, creating environments where people thrive.
