Metal Ceiling

Clip In Tile

Clip-In concealed panels provide durable and monolithic ceilings for interior and can be used in external conditions where the False Ceiling is not directly exposed to rainfall. Clip-In panels are available in a variety of rectangular and square sizes like 600*600mm, 1200*600mm, 1200*300mm, 300*300mm & custom sizes. Modular and circular light fitting and grilles can be integrated within the Clip-In system. Clip-In tiles are ideal for use in areas requiring a high degree of Cleanliness such as Laboratories, Hospitals & Operation Theatres. A totally air & dust tight ceiling can be achieved with a Silicon Sealant applied between the joints of the tiles.

Panel (Galvanized Steel):

Metalium make Clip In Panels 600 * 600 mm area made from 0.45 / 0.50mm TCT Galvanized Steel sheet as per IS-513. Panel shall be square edged. Panel size shall be 600 * 600 mm and shall be coil / powder coated on external surface only.

Panel (Aluminium):

Metalium make Clip In Panels 600 * 600 mm area made from 0.6 / 0.7 mm TCT aluminium alloy like AA-8011. Panel shall be square edged. Panel size shall be 600 * 600 mm and shall be coil / powder coated on external surface only.

Paint Detail:

Coil coated panel shall be chromatized for maximum bond between metal and paint, enamelled twice under high temperature, one side with full primer and finish coat, the other side (Inner side) with a primer coating and skin coat on continuous paint line or Powder coated panel shall be 50 microns thick on exposed surface.

Perforation Details:

2.5mm dia, 5.5mm Centre to Centre in Regular Pattern.

Suspension:

Tiles would be suspended by suspension system comprising 0.45 mm Galvanized Steel clip in profiles at 600 mm centre to centre. Panel shall be snapped into clip in profile. Suspension profiles would be suspended from roof structure by 4 mm Galvanized wire rod and level adjusting spring steel clips and hold on hangers at maximum 1200 centre to centre. When two or more carriers are to be joined, they shall be joined together by means of carrier coupler. Pressure clip shall be provided along the length to hold the panels.

Edge Profile:

Edge profile shall be L- shaped, roll formed out of 0.5mm Galvanized steel / aluminium strips with coil coating or powder Coating in standard colors.
